about this site

My name is Andrew. I live in the United States. I write here when I have something worth saying — which is not always often.

This site is for two things: reviews of products, shows, services, and whatever else I'm paying attention to, and tips — small useful things I've learned that I want to write down somewhere findable.

why does it look like this?

In 1996 I used to sneak onto the card-catalog computers at my county library and read Star Trek fan sites in Lynx, the text-only web browser. Black screen, green text, everything plain. I've never found a design I liked better.

This site has no JavaScript, no tracking, no ads, no cookies. It loads in under a second on a dial-up connection. That's the goal.
