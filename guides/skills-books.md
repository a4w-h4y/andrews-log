Each of these books cleverly mixes theory and practice to provide readers with a concrete set of tools to make what might have felt impossible at least feel like something you can work on and improve with time.

### Inspire Confidence

I start with this one because it might be the most widely applicable book on this list.  The book breaks down the behavior, body language, and habits that other people perceive as charisma or a magnetic personality and gives ways to practice these.  My one caution is that the skills this book teaches aren't a cure-all.  Carrying yourself and interacting with others in a way that inspires confidence won't make you more productive or knowledgeable.  However, if you find you're already bringing value to the table but you're struggling to get others to see that value in work meetings, job interviews, or casual social situations, this book can help you understand why and fix it. 

Book: [The Charisma Myth: How Anyone Can Master the Art and Science of Personal Magnetism](https://www.amazon.com/Charisma-Myth-Science-Personal-Magnetism/dp/1591845947)

### Put Off Procrastination

When I was in high school, I thought that surely by the time I was in college I would learn how to stop procrastinating.  When I was in college, I thought that surely by the time I was in graduate school I would learn how to stop procrastinating.  When I was in law school--well, you get the idea.  This book helps you understand how procrastination isn't a sign of laziness but rather a method we sometimes use to cope with certain kinds of fear (which I hear the kids these days call "anxiety").  While I usually improved enough at whatever I was afraid of failing at to the point where I no longer needed to compulsively put it off in order to avoid judgment, that didn't always keep me from reacting to new and novel challenges with this destructive pattern.  This book helps you recognize the pattern and offers excellent tools and habits for planning your way out of it.

Book: [The Now Habit: A Strategic Program for Overcoming Procrastination and Enjoying Guilt-Free Play](https://www.amazon.com/Now-Habit-Overcoming-Procrastination-Guilt-Free/dp/1585425524)

### Remember

The memory techniques in this book are the closest thing I have ever found to a real-life cheat code.  The book explains the power of association to help you remember people, places, and things.  It then teaches tried and tested methods for creating useful associations (often zany mental images) that enable you to remember names and faces of new people you meet, long lists, sequences, long numbers, and even those seemingly brilliant thoughts that wake you up in the middle of the night.  One of the book's most valuable insights is that none of the techniques are perfect, and some take effort to use.  But they take considerably less effort than memorizing the same information without the help of a sytem.  And even if you don't remember everything all of the time, these techniques are sure to help you remember more than you would have without them.

Book: [The Memory Book](https://www.amazon.com/Memory-Book-Classic-Improving-School/dp/0345410025/)

### Get *Life* Done

"Your mind is for *having* ideas, not for *holding* them."  With admirable technical precision, David Allen's classic productivity book explains how to create an "external mind" and how to habitually maintain it.  One of the book's major strengths is that it essentially repeats the same material three times in a way that's not boring.  It gives an initial overview and explanation of the system.  It then walks the user through step-by-step implementation of the system.  Listened to as an audio book, that part feels like having Allen in the room as your productivity coach.  Finally, it walks through examples illustrating the power of the system's animating principles.  In doing so, it maps out a broader journey of self-awareness that carries the you from the most granular tasks to contemplating (and setting priorities based on) the purpose of your existence.

Book: [Getting Things Done: The Art of Stress-Free Productivity](https://www.amazon.com/Getting-Things-Done-Stress-Free-Productivity/dp/0143126563/)
